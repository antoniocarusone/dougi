Dougi

Get bakin'

A bread dough calculator
built for bakers who care about precision.

Whether you're scaling a recipe, calculating preferment ratios, or dialing in your water temperature, Dougi handles the math so you can focus on the bake.

Recipe

Use the baker's percentages to break out the amount of each ingredients.

Scale by Quantity & Weight

Set how many loaves you're making and the target weight per loaf.

Autolyse

Pre-calculated flour and water weights for your autolyse step alongside your recipe.

Water Temperature

Work out your ideal water temperature based on flour, room, and preferment temps.

Formula Presets

Start from a built-in preset for common dough styles, or dial in your own from scratch.

Preferment & Bassinage

Include or exclude preferment and bassinage in your recipe on the fly.

Yeast Type

Switch between fresh, active dry, and instant yeast and Dougi will adjusts the formula.

Unit System

Work in grams or ounces, Fahrenheit or Celsius, whichever feels like home.

Contextual Info

Hover over items to learn what they mean, and view calculations and ideal ranges.
