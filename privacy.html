Privacy

Effective March 6, 2026

What Dougi Stores

Dougi runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you enter leaves your device. Your recipe inputs, preferences (theme, units, yeast type), and settings are saved to your browser's local storage so they persist between sessions. We have no servers that receive or store your data.

No Accounts, No Tracking

Dougi does not require an account, login, or any personal information. There are no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking of any kind.

Third-Party Services

Dougi loads one external resource — the IBM Plex Mono typeface from Google Fonts. This is a standard font request and does not transmit any personal data. No other third-party services are used within the app.

Offline Use

Dougi is a progressive web app. Once installed, it works fully offline. No network connection is needed to use the calculator.

Your Control

All data lives in your browser. You can clear it at any time through your browser's storage settings. There is nothing to opt out of — we don't collect anything.
